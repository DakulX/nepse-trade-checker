02 — Market Structure Analysis ★ NEW
★ PROFESSIONAL EDGE0/4
Daily chart shows Higher Highs + Higher Lows (uptrend structure)
This single check would have BLOCKED the PURE trade entirely. PURE was making Lower Highs and Lower Lows since February. We bought a bounce in a downtrend. Professional rule: ONLY buy in uptrend structure on daily chart. LH/LL pattern = avoid completely.
→ Daily chart → manually identify last 3 swing highs and 3 swing lows. Each high must be higher than previous high. Each low must be higher than previous low. If any are lower = downtrend = do not buy.
CRITICAL
Weekly chart trend is bullish or neutral (not bearish)
Weekly trend always overrides daily. PURE daily looked good on April 28. PURE weekly was still making lower highs. When daily says buy but weekly says sell — weekly wins every time. The weekly chart prevented nothing. It could have.
→ Switch to 1W (weekly) chart → price must be above weekly EMA 9 OR weekly structure must show HH/HL. If weekly is in clear downtrend = skip even if daily looks great.
CRITICAL
Stock is outperforming NEPSE index (relative strength positive)
If NEPSE up 1% and your stock up 2% = strong. If NEPSE up 1% and stock flat = weak. If NEPSE flat and stock down = very weak. Only buy stocks that are beating the index. Weak stocks in a rising market will crash hardest when market turns.
→ Compare % change of stock vs NEPSE index over last 5 sessions. Stock must be green while NEPSE is green, or stock flat while NEPSE red. NEPSE up + stock down = avoid.
★ S-TIER
Price is above the 52-week midpoint (not near 52-week low)
52-week range tells the story. Stock trading near 52-week high in uptrend = strong. Near 52-week low = falling knife risk. Ideal buy zone: 40–80% of 52-week range (not at extremes).
→ ShareHub → 52W High/Low → calculate midpoint. Current price should be above midpoint. Formula: (52W High - 52W Low) × 0.4 + 52W Low = minimum acceptable level.
