Indicator Guide — Lagging vs Leading
RSI / MACD / BBLAGGINGReact AFTER price moves. Confirm trend but miss early entry.
OBV / VPTLEADINGVolume-based. Rise before price moves. Spot accumulation early.
MFI (Vol-RSI)LEADINGRSI with volume weight. More accurate buy/sell signals than plain RSI.
StochRSIFASTERSignals 3–5 days before regular RSI. Crosses above 0.2 = early buy.
Williams %RLEADINGBelow -80 = oversold. Above -20 = overbought. Signals before RSI.
VWAPREAL-TIMEFair price today. Above VWAP = buyers in control right now.
CandlestickZERO LAGPrice action itself. Hammer, engulfing, doji give instant signals.

03 — Leading Indicators (Early Signals)
LEADING — LESS LAG0/7
These signal BEFORE price moves — earlier entries, better risk/reward
OBV (On-Balance Volume) is rising
OBV adds volume on up days, subtracts on down days. Rising OBV = institutions buying quietly before price moves. This is the #1 early signal.
→ NepseAlpha / TradingView → Add OBV indicator → line must be trending UP and making higher highs
LEADING
MFI (Money Flow Index) is above 50
MFI = volume-weighted RSI. More accurate than plain RSI. Above 50 = money flowing IN. Below 20 = extreme oversold = bounce likely. Above 80 = overbought.
→ Add MFI(14) → value must be above 50 and rising. Below 25 with price stabilising = oversold bounce signal
LEADING
StochRSI has crossed above 0.2 from below
StochRSI is faster than RSI — signals 3–5 days earlier. Crossing above 0.2 (20) = early buy signal. Regular RSI would only confirm this days later.
→ Add Stochastic RSI(14,3,3) → K line must cross above 20 from below (oversold exit)
LEADING
Williams %R is above -80 (exiting oversold zone)
Williams %R below -80 = deeply oversold. When it crosses back above -80 moving toward -20 = early buy signal, usually 2–3 days before RSI confirms it.
→ Add Williams %R(14) → value must be rising above -80 line. Below -80 = wait. Above -80 and rising = entry zone.
LEADING
Price is trading above VWAP
VWAP = today's fair price weighted by volume. Above VWAP = buyers in control right now (zero lag, real-time signal). Below VWAP = sellers winning today.
→ Add VWAP indicator → current price must be ABOVE the VWAP line. Check intraday (1h or 15m chart).
LEADING
Bullish candlestick pattern at support level
Zero lag — pure price action. Hammer at support = strong buy. Bullish engulfing (green candle covers previous red) = buyers taking over. Doji at support = reversal coming.
→ Daily chart → look for: Hammer (long lower wick), Bullish Engulfing, Morning Star, or Doji at key support (S1/MA/BB lower band)
PRICE ACTION
OBV/MFI is rising while price is flat or dipping (bullish divergence)
THIS IS THE MOST POWERFUL SIGNAL. Price going down but OBV going up = hidden buying. Institutions accumulating while retail sells. Price will follow OBV upward soon.
→ Compare OBV line direction vs price direction. If price making lower lows but OBV making higher lows = STRONG buy signal ahead.

EARLY WARNING
MFI has crossed below 50 (or is above 80 = overbought)
MFI below 50 = money flowing OUT. This happens 2–3 days before RSI crosses below 50. MFI above 80 = extreme overbought = reduce position now before reversal.
→ MFI(14) → if crossing from above to below 50 = early sell. If above 80 = sell 50% immediately.
EARLY WARNING
Williams %R has crossed below -20 (entering overbought → reversal)
%R near -20 = overbought. When it turns and crosses below -20 going toward -80 = early sell signal, typically 2–3 days before RSI confirms overbought.
→ Williams %R(14) → if above -20 and starting to fall = early exit zone. Start reducing position.
EARLY WARNING

Zero lag price action signals. Shooting Star (long upper wick at top) = rejected at resistance. Bearish Engulfing (red candle covers green) = sellers taking over. Doji at top = indecision before drop.
→ Daily chart near your target/resistance → look for: Shooting Star, Bearish Engulfing, Evening Star, or large red candle after multiple green days.
