02 — S-Tier Leading Indicators
★ S-TIER — HIGHEST PRIORITY0/7
S-Tier = leads price by 2–5 days. These fire BEFORE price moves.
Weight 2x compared to A-tier signals
OBV (On-Balance Volume) is rising and making higher highs
OBV is the single most reliable leading indicator. It rises when institutions buy quietly before price moves. NGPL OBV was rising weeks before the big move. If OBV up + price flat = accumulation happening.
→ TradingView/NepseAlpha → Add OBV → line must be trending UP and making higher peaks over last 5–10 sessions
★ S-TIER
OBV Bullish Divergence — OBV rising while price is flat or dipping
THE MOST POWERFUL SIGNAL IN THIS ENTIRE CHECKLIST. Price going down but OBV going up = institutions accumulating while retail panics and sells. Price will always follow OBV eventually. This is how PURE set up in early April.
→ Compare OBV direction vs price direction. Price making lower lows but OBV making higher lows = STRONG early buy. Enter before the breakout.
★ DIVERGENCE
MFI (Money Flow Index) is above 50 and rising
MFI = RSI + volume combined. More accurate than plain RSI because it includes actual money flow. Above 50 = money flowing IN. Rising MFI = increasing buying pressure. KBL MFI was falling even when price held = early warning.
→ Add MFI(14) → value above 50 dashed line AND line must be pointing upward. If MFI below 40 = avoid completely.
★ S-TIER
MFI crossed above 50 from below (fresh crossover)
Even stronger than MFI just being above 50. A fresh MFI cross above 50 signals a new wave of buying momentum beginning. This is the entry trigger. Like PURE April 8–10 — MFI crossed 50 and OBV started rising simultaneously.
→ Look for MFI line crossing the 50 horizontal line from below. This crossover + OBV rising = highest conviction buy setup.
★ S-TIER
StochRSI K line crossed above D line AND above 20 (from oversold)
StochRSI is 3–5 days faster than plain RSI. When K (blue/faster line) crosses above D (orange/slower) while both are below 20 = the earliest possible buy signal. This fired on PURE on April 8 — RSI only confirmed on April 27.
→ Add StochRSI(14,14,3,3) → K line must cross above D line while both are below 20. That crossover = early entry signal.
★ S-TIER
Williams %R crossed above -80 (exiting oversold zone)
Williams %R below -80 = deeply oversold. When it crosses back above -80 and heads toward 0 = early buy signal. Fires 2–3 days before RSI confirms. Ideal entry: %R between -80 and -50, still rising.
→ Add Williams %R(14) → value must cross above -80 horizontal line from below. Rising %R between -80 to -50 = sweet spot entry zone.
★ S-TIER
Price is trading above VWAP (today's session)
VWAP = zero lag real-time signal. Above VWAP = buyers in control right now. If price dips and reclaims VWAP with volume = strong intraday buy signal. Below VWAP = sellers winning today, wait.
→ Add VWAP(hlc3, Session) → current price must be ABOVE the VWAP line. Check on 1h or 15m chart for intraday confirmation.

03 — A-Tier Indicators + Market Context
A-TIER — CONFIRMATION0/7
A-tier confirms what S-tier already signalled. Don't buy on A-tier alone.
Heikin Ashi candles are green with no lower wicks
HA candles with no lower shadow = strongest bullish signal. Buyers so strong price never dips intraday. 3+ consecutive green HA candles with no lower wick = confirmed uptrend, safe to hold or add.
→ Switch chart to Heikin Ashi mode → last 3+ candles must be solid green (no lower wick = ideal). Any doji or red HA = trend weakening.
A-TIER
Price is above EMA 9
Short-term trend direction. Price above EMA 9 = upward momentum. Price below = avoid. Works best when EMA 9 is also sloping upward, not flat.
→ Add EMA(9, close) → today's closing price must be above the EMA line. EMA line itself should be rising not flat.
A-TIER
Price is above middle Bollinger Band (BB midline = 20MA)
BB middle = the trend divider. Above = uptrend zone. Below = downtrend zone. Best buys happen when price bounces off the middle BB from below and closes above it with volume.
→ Add BB(20,2) → price must be above the middle orange line. That crossover with volume = buy signal.
A-TIER
Bullish candlestick at key support (Hammer / Engulfing / Doji reversal)
Zero lag pure price action signal. Hammer at support = buyers stepped in hard. Bullish Engulfing = green candle covers previous red = sellers defeated. Morning Star at bottom = 3-candle reversal pattern.
→ Regular candle chart → at S1/BB lower/EMA support → look for: long lower wick (hammer), green candle larger than prev red (engulfing), or 3-candle morning star pattern.
PRICE ACTION
NEPSE index is green today (+0.5% or more)
Market-wide tide lifts all boats. Buying in a broadly red market adds unnecessary risk even for good stocks. Wait for a green market day for best entries.
→ NepseAlpha or ShareSansar homepage → NEPSE index must show green with +0.5% or more today.
CONTEXT
Buy queue total is larger than sell queue total
Order book shows real-time buying pressure. More buyers queued = price pressure upward. Like NGPL Apr 7: 1.14L buy vs 91K sell = bullish. Confirmed the trade.
→ Broker TMS or NepseAlpha Market Depth → Total Qty Buy must be larger than Total Qty Sell.
ORDER BOOK
Today's volume is above the 30-day average
High volume = conviction. Low volume green days reverse easily. Volume bar today must be taller than the average of recent bars. Volume confirmation is what separates real moves from fakeouts.
→ Daily chart volume bars → today's bar must be visually taller than the average of recent 20–30 bars. Or use Volume MA(30) indicator.

02 — S-Tier Early Sell Warnings (Leading)
★ S-TIER — ACT EARLY0/4
These warn you 2–5 days BEFORE price drops — exit while price is still high
OBV Bearish Divergence — price making higher highs but OBV declining
THE EARLIEST AND MOST POWERFUL SELL SIGNAL. Price still going up but OBV falling = smart money distributing quietly at high prices. Retail is buying while institutions are selling. Price will follow OBV down. This is exactly what happened to SOHL and HFIN before their eventual reversals.
→ Compare OBV direction vs price. Price at new high but OBV lower than previous high = SELL at least 50% immediately. Don't wait for price to confirm.
★ DIVERGENCE
MFI crossed below 50 OR MFI above 80 (overbought)
MFI crossing below 50 = money flowing out, 2–3 days before price drops. MFI above 80 = extreme overbought = reduce by 50% now. Like PURE StochRSI at 99 on Apr 28 = short term exhaustion signal.
→ MFI(14) → if above 80: sell 50% now. If crossing from above to below 50: sell 50–70% immediately. These are early warnings before the drop is visible in price.
★ S-TIER
StochRSI K crossed below D from above 80 (overbought exit)
StochRSI K crossing below D while both above 80 = momentum peaked and reversing. This fires 3–5 days before price starts to fall. Like PURE StochRSI K at 99 = extreme overbought = consolidation or dip incoming very soon.
→ StochRSI → when K line (blue) crosses below D line (orange) while both are above 80 = reduce position by 50%. Price drop coming in days.
★ S-TIER
Williams %R above -20 and starting to turn down
%R near 0 = deeply overbought. When it peaks above -20 and starts falling = early sell signal, fires 2–3 days before RSI would show overbought. Don't wait for RSI — act when %R turns from -20 zone.
→ Williams %R(14) → when value is above -20 and line starts declining = early exit signal. Sell partial. Full exit when price confirms by closing below EMA 9.
